Welcome To EMTRANS

EMTRANS is an online bus booking that is designed for Accra Technical University employees to provide a bus transportation system,a facility to reserved seats,cancellation of seats and different types of enquiry which need an instant and quick reservation.It is a web software solution designed to provide employees with a personalized easy-to-utilize user experience for booking and purchasing tickets online. It stores employees's data,scheduled routes,and other information.
